Real Boss Moves

What's That About? Why Real?

This is something else that I am passionate about. I am going after my interests in coding and working on using the skills I have learned to help market myself. Real Boss Moves was initially just for me. I wanted something that sounded strong. It needed to be something that did not sound far fetched. It needed to be possible or achievable.

The B.O.S.S. Blog This stands for Be Optimistic, Strong, & Self-Confident. That is what I think of when I think of a 'BOSS'.

When I say BOSS I don't mean like someone's boss on the job. No, that is asking too much. No, I need people to work on being their own version of a superhero to themselves. I need them to 'Boss Up' in their own lives. I need them to show up and show out in every aspect of their lives and revel in what makes them them. I want to write about my journey, what inspires me, and hopefully help others in the same situation to be encouraged to take charge of their own lives either a bit a time or the whole shebang.

What about the Moves? The moves are all on the individual. If you don't take steps to change your life who will do it for you?

I needed the name to have momentum, and it needed to be doable in the "now" if you pardon the phrase. Here I plan to focus on ways to move, my own health journey, and what others are doing to make their lives better. What do they do to 'Boss Up' daily?
